Mobile Phones have revolutionized the way we communicate. For the older generation of mobile phone users, the options can be overwhelming and the skills needed to utilize the features, confusing. This infographic helps provide guidance in selecting the right type of mobile phones, considering their physical and psychological need, thereby helping them be more independent and live with dignity.
